About Us

At Artoraa India, we believe that every piece of art tells a story, and our mission is to connect exceptional artworks with collectors who appreciate their true value. We are curators of artistic legacies, delivering a personalized experience that transforms buying and selling art into a refined journey.

With a commitment to transparency and fairness, our fee structure is straightforward: a single fee, based on a sliding scale from 2.5% to 15%, covers all aspects of our services, including research, negotiations, and logistical arrangements. Our ‘no transaction, no fee’ policy ensures that you only pay when a deal is successfully concluded, and we exclusively represent your interests in every negotiation.

Advisory Services for Auctions

Whether you're entering the auction market for the first time or are a seasoned buyer or seller, Artoraa India offers expert guidance and tailored services to help you navigate the complexities of auctions. Fees for these services vary based on the level of assistance required, ensuring that you receive exactly the support you need.

Managing Collections

We provide premium collection management services to our longstanding clients, while new clients can access these on an hourly basis. Our team ensures that every artwork in your collection is handled with the utmost care and professionalism.

Additional Services & Costs

We are committed to transparency in all external costs related to art care, including evaluations, framing, transport, and conservation. These expenses are always agreed upon in advance to ensure a seamless and stress-free experience. For personal consultations or artwork viewings, any travel arrangements are handled with the same precision and attention to detail that defines all of our services.

At Artoraa India, we elevate the art transaction process, making it not only effortless but exceptional—where passion for art meets sophistication.
